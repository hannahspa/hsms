# MySpa Reference Notes

> Ghi chu quan sat tu `https://hannah.myspa.vn` de HSMS mo phong flow desktop, khong copy may moc giao dien.

## Nguyen tac su dung MySpa lam tham chieu

- MySpa la tham chieu ve luong thao tac va cach nhan vien quen su dung.
- HSMS khong lam lai tu dau; tiep tuc tren kien truc hien co.
- HSMS giu Hannah Luxury design: espresso sidebar, champagne accent, bang du lieu ro, thao tac nhanh.
- Desktop Admin/POS uu tien truoc. Mobile check-in va iPad menu lam sau.
- Tat ca ghi chu ben duoi uu tien desktop viewport 1440x900. Ghi chu mobile/tablet cu chi dung de tham khao phu.

## Desktop shell MySpa

Quan sat desktop:

- Sidebar trai co dinh, nen toi, nhom menu ro.
- Topbar ngang co:
  - Ten spa/branch selector.
  - Loi chao nguoi dung.
  - Tim nhanh KH.
  - CTA nhanh: `Tao don hang`, `Them KH`.
- Content nam ben phai, nen sang, trang danh sach uu tien bang.
- Footer/hotline ho tro co dinh phia duoi.

Dieu HSMS can hoc:

- Admin desktop nen giu sidebar + topbar nhu hien tai, nhung can tinh chat thao tac nhanh giong MySpa.
- Tim nhanh KH va nut Tao don nen luon de thay tren desktop.
- Khong uu tien card lon cho danh sach van hanh; table-first moi dung flow quan ly.

## Man hinh danh sach ban hang MySpa desktop

Quan sat chinh:

- Tieu de: Danh sach ban hang.
- Nut tao don hang nam dau trang.
- Tim kiem nhanh theo ma don/khuyen mai hoac ten/so dien thoai khach.
- Co nut `Tim kiem nang cao`.
- Table-first.
- Cot chinh:
  - Ma don hang / Ngay gio
  - Khach hang
  - Trang thai
  - NV thuc hien / Tong Commission
  - Giuong
  - Don gia
  - VAT
  - Giam gia
  - Tong tien thanh toan / Cong no
  - Duoc tao boi / Chi nhanh / Ma don chi nhanh
  - Nen tang
  - Ghi chu
  - Action
- Trang thai thay duoc:
  - Cho thanh toan
  - Da thanh toan
- Don su dung the lieu trinh co nhan `Lieu trinh` va ghi chu dang:
  - Su dung the lieu trinh [ten dich vu] [da_dung]/[tong]. Ma the: THE-LT-...
- Action cuoi dong gom xem/sua, in, thong tin, huy/xoa.

Dieu HSMS can hoc:

- Danh sach don hang can du cot de doi soat, khong chi la card gon.
- Trang thai don va nhan `Lieu trinh` phai ro.
- Commission can hien ngay tren danh sach.
- Cong no phai la cot rieng, khong an sau.

Trang thai HSMS ngay 2026-05-21:

- Da doi sang table desktop theo MySpa/Hannah: ma don/ngay gio, khach hang, trang thai, NV/Tour/HH, don gia, VAT, giam gia, tong tien/thanh toan/cong no, nguoi tao/chi nhanh/ma CN, nen tang, ghi chu, action. Cot `Giuong` da bo theo quy trinh Hannah Spa hien tai.
- Da hien Tour va HH rieng theo nhan vien ngay tren danh sach.
- Da hien TEST/POS va tien thu/cong no de doi soat nhanh.
- Bang co horizontal scroll ben trong card, khong day vo khung trang.
- POS tao don co 2 tang giam gia nhu MySpa: giam tung dich vu tren dong DV/SP va giam tong don o khu tong ket.
- PTTT tao don dung dropdown nhu MySpa/Hannah mong muon, hien ro `Chuyen Khoan - MB Bank` va `Quet The - TP Bank`. Logic chi tinh tien da thu khi dong thanh toan co PTTT hop le.
- Man tao don da doi catalog tu list sang card grid giong MySpa desktop: search pill, tab Dich vu/San pham, category chips, card dich vu co ten/thoi luong/gia.
- Rieng POS ep sidebar ve dang icon compact de tang khong gian thao tac, giong MySpa POS desktop hon.
- Panel phai `Thong tin don hang` da can lai theo MySpa: header tim, tab Don hang/Vat tu tieu hao ben phai, khoi Khach le + Ngay gio + Chi nhanh, header bang 4 cot DV/SP - SL - Giam gia - Thanh tien, trang thai rong chi hien o `Them chu ho KH`, khong show thanh toan khi chua co item.
- Bottom action bar trong POS doi sang nen tim giong MySpa voi cac nut quay lai, Luu, Thanh toan, Thanh toan & in.
- Con thieu module that cho `VAT`, `Ma don chi nhanh`, va action in hoa don. Tam thoi de `—` de khong tao du lieu gia.
